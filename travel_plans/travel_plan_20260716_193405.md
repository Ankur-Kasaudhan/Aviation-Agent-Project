## 🗓️ Itinerary
Based on your query, I've created a 4-day travel itinerary for a budget-friendly trip to Goa in September end. Since you're looking for a budget trip, I've considered affordable options for accommodations, food, and activities.

**Day 1: Arrival and Exploration of North Goa**

* Arrive at Dabolim Airport, Goa
* Check-in at a budget-friendly hotel in Calangute or Baga, such as Zostel or a similar hostel (approx. ₹1,500 - ₹2,000 per night)
* Visit the famous Calangute Beach and Baga Beach
* Explore the local market and try some street food (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person for dinner)
* Nightlife: Head to Tito's Lane or Club Cubana for a fun night out (entry fees and drinks approx. ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 per person)

**Day 2: South Goa Expedition**

* Start the day with breakfast at a local café (approx. ₹200 - ₹500 per person)
* Visit the stunning Palolem Beach and Colva Beach
* Take a boat ride to see dolphins (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person)
* Lunch at a local restaurant (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person)
* Visit the historic Benaulim Church and the nearby Madgaon Market
* Return to North Goa in the evening and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person)

**Day 3: Water Sports and Relaxation**

* Start the day with water sports at Calangute Beach, such as parasailing, jet-skiing, or banana boat ride (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,500 per person)
* Relax on the beach or by the pool at your hotel
* Lunch at a beach shack (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person)
* Visit the famous Anjuna Flea Market (every Wednesday) or the Saturday Night Market (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person for shopping and food)
* Nightlife: Head to a beach party or a local bar for a relaxed evening (approx. ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 per person)

**Day 4: Cultural Experience and Departure**

* Visit the historic Fort Aguada and the nearby lighthouse (approx. ₹50 - ₹100 per person)
* Explore the local village and try some traditional Goan cuisine for lunch (approx. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person)
* Visit the famous Se Cathedral and the Basilica of Bom Jesus in Old Goa (approx. ₹50 - ₹100 per person)
* Depart for the airport and head back home

**Budget Breakdown:**

* Accommodation (3 nights): ₹4,500 - ₹6,000 (avg. ₹1,500 - ₹2,000 per night)
* Food and drinks: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 (avg. ₹2,000 - ₹3,000 per day)
* Transportation: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 (depending on the mode of transport and frequency of use)
* Activities and entry fees: ₹2,000 - ₹4,000 (avg. ₹500 - ₹1,000 per person per activity)
